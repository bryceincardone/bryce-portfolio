Projects

TSMC (Nov 2022 - Present)

Our responsibilities encompass a comprehensive approach to managing the plumbing and pipefitting aspects of abatement at TSMC, including drainage, dosing, and tool hookups. Drainage involves designing and implementing systems to effectively remove waste liquids and contaminants from the manufacturing area, ensuring a clean and safe working environment. Dosing entails the precise measurement and delivery of chemicals or additives required for the abatement process, ensuring optimal performance and compliance with environmental standards. Tool hookups involve connecting abatement equipment to the manufacturing tools or machinery, ensuring seamless integration and efficient operation of the abatement systems within the manufacturing process. By overseeing these critical components, we contribute to the overall success of the abatement process at TSMC, mitigating environmental impact and ensuring the continued operation of semiconductor manufacturing processes.
